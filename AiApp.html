Identity Practice Generator

Small repeated behaviors shape identity. Choose the kind of person you want to become, then generate one action or habit that aligns with that direction.

How This Works

Lasting change happens through repeated behavior. This app is built on the idea that the actions we take—whether small habits or one-time decisions—gradually shape our identity.

Instead of focusing only on goals, this approach emphasizes practicing the behaviors that reflect the kind of person you want to become. Over time, these patterns influence how you think, act, and see yourself.

Choose an identity and a difficulty level, and the app will generate a behavior along with a reinforcement message tied to who you are becoming.
